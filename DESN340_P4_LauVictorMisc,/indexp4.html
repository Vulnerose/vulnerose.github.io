About Us

We aim to create inviting atmospheres that are both peaceful and vibrant, where people can relax and socialize. Each pop-up location has a distinct colour scheme, but we always include our friendly ghost mascot to maintain brand consistency. Our spaces are decorated with crayons, adding a playful element, and emit a comforting ambiance with the pleasant aromas of freshly brewed coffee and lavender.
